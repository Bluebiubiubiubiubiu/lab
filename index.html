Research Areas

Molecular Biology

In-depth study of intracellular molecular mechanisms, exploring the relationship between protein structure and function to provide new targets for disease treatment.

  • Protein folding and function
  • Enzyme catalysis mechanisms
  • Intermolecular interactions

Genetic Engineering

Utilizing advanced technologies like CRISPR-Cas9 for gene editing, developing novel gene therapy methods and biopharmaceutical technologies.

  • CRISPR gene editing
  • Gene therapy vectors
  • Synthetic biology

Biotechnology Innovation

Developing novel biosensors, diagnostic technologies, and therapeutic methods to advance precision medicine and personalized treatment.

  • Biosensor development
  • Nanobiotechnology
  • Precision medicine applications
